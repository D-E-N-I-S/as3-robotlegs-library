Classpublic class Utilities
InheritanceUtilities Object

Utility to map dependencies of certain utilities.

Probably the best place to define your mappings is in the mapInjections method of your Context. You can simply call the method mapDepedencies of Utilities to determine all your mappings for the following utilities.

  • AssetLoader
  • Layers
  • Localizer
  • Navigator
  • Router
  • Styles
